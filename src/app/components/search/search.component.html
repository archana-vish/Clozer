FAQ
What is Clozer?
Clozer is a platform to help home buyers find a suitable area to settle down based on personal preferences.
How is this different from Rightmove or Zoopla then?
Rightmove and Zoopla are platforms where you go search for properties once you know where you want to live. Clozer is few stages prior to that. We help you identify areas that might be suitable and then use this to find properties using Rightmove or Zoopla.
Clozer helps answer questions such as- "I work in Reading and my partner works in London Canary Wharf. We have a 2 year old. Our maximum budget is $600,000. What areas should we consider assuming both of us dont want to spend more than 45 mins traveling to work?"
But I could ask my friends or colleagues and shortlist areas?
Yes, that is an option. But if you get 15 recommendations, how do you isloate personal bias and look at these areas objectively? How do you know you are not missing areas that could be better suited for you and your family?
How should I use Clozer?
Tell Clozer where you work (or some reference postcode), your budget and your maximum commute time/distance. Clozer will shortlist areas & sectors and rank them based on a score calculated using several attributes. The score and the shortlisted areas can change if you modify your search parameters. This is because Clozer evaluates and scores individual areas by comparing against all shortlisted areas.
How is this Clozer Score calculated?
Assume for your search criteria, Clozer has shortlisted 10 areas. An independent score for these 10 areas is calculated using factors such as House Prices, Crime, Travel, Schools and Facilities (high street shops, gas stations, grocery stores, etc.). The scoring is relative (i.e) the scoring is specific to these 10 areas and is used to rank the suitability of the 20 areas based on your search criteria.
What do you mean by relative ranking/scoring?
Assume you change your search criteria (eg: increase travel time) and now we have 15 areas. These 15 areas are compared and a relative score is assigned. If an area previously had a score of 200, it could drop because another newly found area is better. This helps Clozer rank and provide suggestions most suited to you.
